What's new?

We encounter so much information every day, and sometimes it's just too much.

"What's new?" is a project to help you with that by sending a summarization of what's up on Medium and other news platforms, with the hope you can better pick the best information you want to learn more!

Two features are summarizing any news articles and summarizing medium articles in any given time period.
